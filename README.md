## Selected work

### [Financial Risk News Classification](https://github.com/ezeriosk/financial-risk-news-analysis)

**Business question:** How can risk teams prioritise relevant financial news without relying on an opaque model?

An interpretable NLP pipeline using TF-IDF, cosine similarity and transparent business rules. The output is designed for monitoring and review, with synthetic data, automated tests, an executed notebook and a dashboard.

### [Ticket Demand Forecasting](https://github.com/ezeriosk/ticket-demand-forecasting)

**Business question:** How should an operations team anticipate demand and plan capacity?

An operational analytics case study using 60,000 synthetic tickets. It compares Holt-Winters and SARIMA forecasts while connecting demand peaks, SLA performance and team bottlenecks to staffing decisions.

### [Carsharing Pricing Analysis](https://github.com/ezeriosk/carsharing-pricing-analysis)

**Product question:** Where does upfront-pricing accuracy break down, and what should product and operations teams fix first?

An analysis of 2,875 orders connecting GPS quality, geography and application versions with price variation and customer impact, ending with prioritised product and modelling recommendations.
